Red Sauce Pasta

Description

This delicious Red Sauce Pasta is a classic!

Ingredients

  • 1 box of pasta
  • 1 jar of marinara sauce

Steps

  1. Boil 3 cups water on the stovetop
  2. Add pasta and let boil for 12 minutes, stirring occasionally. Drain
  3. Heat the sauce on the stovetop or in the microwave
  4. Mix together
