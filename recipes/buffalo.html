Vegan Buffalo Chicken Dip

Description

A plant-based creamy buffalo chicken dip. The "chicken" is actually artichokes!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups raw cashews
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup buffalo sauce
  • (1) 14-ounce can artichoke hearts, drained

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F
  2. Soak the cashews: Heat up about 3 cups of water in a tea kettle or small pot. Pour over cashews and let them soak for 5 minutes. Drain
  3. Blend the drained cashews, water, and seasoning until very smooth
  4. Add buffalo sauce and artichokes. Pulse a couple of times but do not blend. You want it chunky!
  5. Transfer to an oven safe dish and bake for 25-30 minutes
